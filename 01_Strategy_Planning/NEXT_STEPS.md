# 🗺️ Next 10 Steps: Auto Repair

This roadmap outlines the strategic priorities for the development and growth of the Auto Repair business.

1. 🔧 **Facility Selection & Leasing:** Identify and secure a 2,500+ sq ft facility with high-traffic visibility and appropriate zoning for automotive work.
2. 🛠️ **Equipment Procurement:** Purchase and install heavy-duty hydraulic lifts, advanced diagnostic scanners, and comprehensive specialized tool sets.
3. 📜 **Licensing & Permits:** Obtain all necessary state-specific automotive repair licenses, environmental permits for fluid disposal, and robust business insurance.
4. 👨‍🔧 **Staff Recruitment:** Hire experienced, ASE-certified mechanics and a customer-centric service advisor to manage client relations.
5. 📦 **Parts Supplier Integration:** Establish wholesale accounts with major parts distributors to ensure rapid delivery and competitive pricing.
6. 💻 **Shop Management Software:** Implement an integrated system for digital work orders, parts inventory, invoicing, and customer history tracking.
7. 📢 **Local SEO & Marketing:** Launch a professional website, optimize the Google Business Profile, and initiate local search ads targeting repair keywords.
8. 🧼 **Operational Workflow Setup:** Define and document Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for vehicle intake, multi-point inspections, and final quality control.
9. 🤝 **Fleet Service Outreach:** Identify and pitch maintenance contracts to local businesses with vehicle fleets, such as delivery services or contractors.
10. 🚀 **Grand Opening Event:** Host a community "Open House" featuring free basic inspections, introductory discounts, and local refreshments to build awareness.
